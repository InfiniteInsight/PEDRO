🦝 Welcome to Pedro!

Before we get started, here's exactly what Pedro does (and doesn't do) with your data.

Your Tasks Stay on YOUR Device

All your tasks, photos, and settings are stored locally in your browser using localStorage. Nothing is sent to any server. We don't have accounts, analytics, or tracking. Pedro is 100% client-side.

Photo Input

Photos you attach to tasks are resized and stored locally as data in your browser. They never leave your device.

⚠️ Voice Input — Please Read

Voice input uses your browser's built-in Web Speech API. This means:

  • Your audio is sent to Google's servers (Chrome/Edge) or Apple's servers (Safari) for processing
  • Pedro has no control over how these companies store or use your audio
  • Your spoken words may be retained, reviewed, or used for training by these companies
  • This is not private — treat it like talking to Siri or Google Assistant
  • Voice input requires an internet connection

If you're uncomfortable with this, leave voice input disabled. Text and photo input work perfectly without it.
